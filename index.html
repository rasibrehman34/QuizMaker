About Us

This dynamic quiz-making website was meticulously developed under the esteemed guidance of Prof. Muhammad Abubakar. His mentorship provided us with invaluable insights, enabling us to refine our vision and transform it into an interactive and engaging platform. His expertise played a pivotal role in shaping the core functionalities and overall structure of the project.

Our dedicated team, comprising Muhammad Hashir Anwar and Rasib Rehman, worked collaboratively to conceptualize, design, and implement innovative features. From the Single Player Mode, which offers an immersive solo challenge, to the Double Player Mode, which fosters competitive engagement, every component was crafted with precision. Our goal was to create a seamless, intuitive experience that blends learning with excitement, making knowledge exploration both accessible and enjoyable.
This project is a testament to our unwavering commitment, teamwork, and passion for technological innovation.
